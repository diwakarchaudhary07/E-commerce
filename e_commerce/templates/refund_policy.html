We want you to be satisfied with every purchase. If you are not completely happy with your order, our refund policy outlines how to submit a request and what to expect.

Refund Eligibility

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our support team with your order number.
  • Provide details about the issue and attach photos if applicable.
  • Ship the item back to our returns address if requested.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the refund approval. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.
